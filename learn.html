Learning the Ukelele

Learning the ukulele is a fantastic hobby and an excellent introduction to the world of music. It's easy to pick up, fun to play, and a great way to develop musical skills. Joining a group like the Sou'West Strummers or another ukulele club gives you the opportunity to practice for two hours in a supportive and enjoyable environment. You'll not only improve your playing but also receive valuable advice from experienced musicians. Plus, being part of a club helps you stay motivated, meet new people, and enjoy the social side of making music together.


Check out the Songs for inspiration, can't decide? type "pick" in search, or if your learning use "learn" for songs that contain only the easy cords - A, Am, C, C7, G, F
Note that a few songs with D and G7 are also present, you can use the last chord again or G instead of G7
